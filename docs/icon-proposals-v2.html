Blisko — Icon Proposals v2

5 variations on the "b + Signal" direction. Cream background, bold serif letterform, coral signal arcs — exploring weight, placement, and accent variations.

Original #03 — b + Signal
Lowercase 'b' in classic serif on cream, coral broadcast arcs top-right. The starting point for all 5 new variations below.
Proposal 3A
Bold Weight
Larger, heavier 'b' dominating the frame. Thicker signal arcs. More presence, more confidence.
Proposal 3B
Inner Beacon
Coral dot inside the counter of the 'b' with ripple rings radiating outward. The signal comes from within.
Proposal 3C
Dark Edition
Inverted — cream 'b' on near-black with soft coral arcs. Elegant night-mode feel. Stands out on light wallpapers.
Proposal 3D
Outline + Pin
Stroked 'b' with coral blush in the counter and a pin dot. Two arcs. Lighter, more airy — refined and modern.
Proposal 3E
Sweeping Arc
Big sweeping quarter-circle arcs wrapping from right to top with a terminating dot. Dramatic, dynamic energy.
